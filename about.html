About Us

Pioneer Design & Development Limited (PDDL) has established at November 2013.At first its name was Pioneer Design & Development. Its initial work was only Engineering Consultancy. But now construction and Real Estate is also it’s running project. Actually, PDDL works on consultancy and Real Estate, and Matrix Construction for construction work. Matrix Construction has completed new construction and retrofitting works especially in RMG sector. At present PDDL is a complete company for a total solution.

Matrix Construction about us

Matrix Construction is Bangladesh’s one of the leading engineering consultancy, construction firm in Bangladesh. We are currently providing design (such as- Architectural design, Interior design, Electrical design, and Structural design), Matrix construction and other services to ensure high quality life. Our quality of materials and equipment that we use on assets are outstanding and environment friendly. We have skilled engineers who not only enable the safety of installation but also verify, assess, design and draw the structures in a way that prevent seismic vulnerability. Our committed, goal oriented and independent very own engineering team can conduct any structural design, make flexible adjustment, assessment and evaluation for that architectural, electrical and structural design. In RMG sector we have already completed Matrix construction that offers new construction and retrofitting works. We are special on assisting our client on gigantic construction projects where our service contain the ability to manage and mitigate the risk related to any structure. Our team’s ultimate goal is to enhance the performance of the project by improving the quality, reducing project delays and minimizing re-work for any project.
